1.Art, in its broadest sense, is a form of communication. It means whatever the artist intends it to mean, and this meaning is shaped by the materials, techniques, and forms it makes use of, as well as the ideas and feelings it creates in its viewers . Art is an act of expressing feelings, thoughts, and observations.
2.Paintings - Painting is the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface (support base).
3.Prints - A print is a two-dimensional art form created by an impression made by a method involving a transfer from one surface to another.
4.Drawings -As with paintings, drawings are one of the oldest art forms around and can be traced back to prehistoric times.
5.Craft - The art of craft-making can be defined as an occupation or trade which involves manual dexterity and skilled artistry.
